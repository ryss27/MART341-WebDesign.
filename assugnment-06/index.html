Cajun Shrimp Alfredo

Ingredients

  • 1 LB Shrimp
  • 1 TBS Cajun Seasoning
  • 4 TBS Butter
  • 1 TSP Olive Oil
  • 1 TBS Minced Garlic
  • 3 TBS Flour
  • 2 Cups Milk
  • 1 Cup Pasta Water
  • 8 OZ Fettuccine Noodles
  • 1 Cups Grated Parmesan
  • 1 TSP Salt
  • Chopped Parsley (Optional)

Directions

  1. Cook fettuccine noodles according to package directions.
  2. Drain & toss with a bit of olive oil, and set aside. Remember to reserve 1 Cup of Pasta Water!
  3. In a large skillet melt 1 tablespoon butter. Stir in 1 teaspoon olive oil
  4. Add shrimp to the skillet and sprinkle Cajun seasoning over the shrimp. Use tongs or a slotted spoon to transfer shrimp to a dish and cover to keep warm.
  5. Melt butter over medium heat. Stir in garlic for 1 minute til fragrant.
  6. Stir in flour for 1 minute. Gradually whisk in milk, then pasta water.
  7. Add parmesan cheese and salt and stir until parmesan is dissolved.
  8. Add cooked fettuccine noodles to the skillet and return shrimp to the skillet.
  9. Garnish with parsley if desired This is an optional step
  10. Serve
