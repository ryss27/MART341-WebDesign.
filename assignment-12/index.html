Boygenius

Boygenius is a band composed of artists Phoebe Bridgers, Lucy Dacus, and Julien Baker.

Facts about the band

  • The band originally came together after Julien Baker met Lucy Dacus at a show where they instantly clicked. A month later, Lucy, Phoebe, and Julien found out they would all soon be touring together. This is when they started writing together.
  • The band's name reflects a critisim on patriacharl manifestations about how men are rewarded and applauded for nearly every move they make, whereas women have to jump through a significant more amount of hoops often for half the recognition.
  • The band has a new album, The Record, that will be coming out on March 31st. This is their first collaboration since their 2018 EP.
